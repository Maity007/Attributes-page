Dr. Norman Borlaug

The man who saved a billion lives

Here's time line of Dr. Borlaug's life:

  • 1914- Bron in Cresco, lowa
  • 1933- Leaves his family's farm to attend the University of Minnesota, thanks to a Depression era programme known as the "National Youth Administration"
  • 1935- Has to stop school and save up more money . Works in the Civilian Conervation Corps, helping straving Americans. "I saw how food changes them", he said. "All of this left scars on me."
  • 1937- finishes university and takes a job in the US Forestry Service
  • 1938- Marries wife of 69 years Margret Gibson. Gers laid off due to budget cuts.
  • 1941- Tries to enroll in the military after the peatl Harbot attack, but is rejected. Insted, the military asked his lab to work on waterproof glue, DDT to control malaria, disinfectants, and other applied science.
  • 2009- dies at the age of 95.
